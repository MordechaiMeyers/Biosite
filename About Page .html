About Page

Oren Meyers grew up in Silver Spring, Maryland and now lives in Cleveland, Ohio with his wife and five kids. Dr. Oren Meyers has a doctorate in Clinical Psychology from Fordham University. His internship training was at the Veterans Affairs Medical Center in Cleveland and Brecksville, OH and his post-doctoral training was at University Hospitals of Cleveland in the Division of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry. Oren serves on the faculty of the Department of Psychiatry at Case Western Reserve University School of Medicine and is licensed by the State of Ohio and the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. Oren also worked several jobs as a consultant in patient centered outcomes. Dr. Meyers worked at Truven Health Analytics as senior research leader and his responsibilities included Senior Research Leader for Clinical Outcome Assessment (PRO, ClinRO, ObsRO) in the Strategic Consulting group at Truven Health Analytics (formerly Thomson Reuters Healthcare). Bringing the value of outcome assessment to medical product development programs at the regulatory, reimbursement, and communications levels. He also worked at Genentech as a consultant in patient centered outcomes and he Led clinical outcomes assessment (COA) activities for select molecules, including developing new measures, developing and executing analytic plans, implementing COAs in clinical trial programs and gathering evidence needed for submission to regulatory authorities. Oren Meyers currently works as a director of patient centered outcome in oncology at GSK.
